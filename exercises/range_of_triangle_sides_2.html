\left\{\begin{array}{3} a+J>a \\ J+a>a \\ a+a>J\end{array}\right.

但 a+J>a ，J+a>a 自動成立，

\left\{\begin{array}{3} J+J>a \\ J+a>J \\ J+a>J\end{array}\right.
